Liber Meletis

Meletis

Meletis is the city of thinkers and scholars. It's built along the Siren Sea and is known for its innovation and magic learning. The city values education, intellect, and the study of gods above anything else. Its streets are built from beautiful patterned bricks and temples to the gods are found in every corner.

The citizens of Meletis are mostly humans, but most outsiders are welcome, as long as they have good intentions. Titons, centaurs, and sometimes satyrs live or trade there. Philosophers, scholars, and thaumaturges are highly respected, and magic is considered to be a gift from the gods and an art. Thecity is protected by the Reverent Army, which aims to spread the word of the gods and defend the polis.

The Council

The city is ruled by The Twelve, a council of philosophers who guide Meletis. They debate about law, education, and the use of magic, and each of the councilers have their own ethics and interests.

Locations

Some of the most well-known places in Meletis include the Dekatia, which is a massive academy for magic and philosophy, the Observatory, a place where you can study Nyx, and the Harbor, which is the trading hub of Meletis.
